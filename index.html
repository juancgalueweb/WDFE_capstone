A little bit about me

I am a Venezuelan guy living in Chile, and an electrical engineer with more than 14 years in the industry, specifically in power generation and distribution, but since the pandemic I started to realize that I wanted to do something differente with my life. I found programming a fascinated subject in the highschool but somehow I lost the path and now I want to reconnect with my passion for programming. I want to become a Front-end Web Developer and master JS and then learn React. Later I want to explore the Back-end.
